Hints to solve ordering of words

  • Find out the topic addressed by the sentences.
  • The sentences starting with the name of a person, the that sentence will be the first in the paragraph.
  • The sentences starting with pronouns other than I and You, then it would not be the first sentence.
  • The sentences starting with the 'T' words other than The, then it will not be the first sentence.
  • If the articles such as 'a','an' or 'the', then that sentence has the probability of being the first sentence.
  • If there are sentences starting with 'But', 'So', 'Now', then they are arranged respectively.
  • The simple, compound and complex sentences are ordered respectively.
